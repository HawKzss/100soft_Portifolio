About

I am a Tech Enthusiast and passionate designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for visual storytelling. My work focuses on creating engaging and impactful designs that communicate ideas effectively. With a strong foundation in design principles and a diverse skill set, I thrive on collaborating with clients to bring their visions to life. Whether it’s branding, marketing materials, or digital content, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality designs that resonate with audiences and elevate brands..

Experience

WAKUE ORG

Brand Designer

In my role as a brand designer at WAKUE ORG, I developed and implemented the visual identity of the organization. I created marketing materials and social media content that aligned with the brand's values, ensuring consistency and enhancing brand recognition. Collaborating with cross-functional teams, I contributed to effective branding strategies that resonated with our target audience.

SCENTSATIONAL STORE

Brand Designer

Developed and executed the brand's visual identity, creating cohesive and engaging designs that reflect the store's ethos. Collaborated with marketing and product teams to design promotional materials, social media content, and packaging. Ensured brand consistency across all platforms, contributing to increased brand recognition and customer engagement.

Education

Self Taught Graphic Designer

Developed skills in graphic design through online courses, mastering tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Photopea, GIMP, and Inkscape. Focused on creating visually compelling designs and enhancing my understanding of design principles.

Self Taught Website Designer

Gained expertise in web design through self-directed learning, utilizing online resources to understand HTML, CSS, and responsive design. Committed to creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing websites that enhance user experience.
